OVERVIEW

I am Tom Angelo Fuerte Fernando, born on September 10, 2001. I am currently living in Moonwalk, Parañaque City in my aunt's residence. I am an optimistic, motivating, and aspiring student of Polytechnic University of the Philippines - Parañaque Campus. Currently the president of the Association of Information and Communicaitions Technology Students (AICTS), I ensure that I fulfill my roles effectively as a student, as a leader, and as a human created by our Lord.

I have chosen my current course, Bachelor of Science in Information Technology because it is nearer to my dream course, BS in Computer Science, which is not available in our campus. It is not BSIT is my last resort but I find it fun. It is because I am fond of software rather than hardware like a Computer Engineering student does. This time, if I am given a chance to study again, I prefer to take Master of Information Technology because it is more preferred in the field than Master of Science in IT, which are for professors.

I am indebted to my late mother who raised me to be who am I today. She has been the one who molded me to be optimistic, joyful, positive-thinking, and productive. In fact, I always wanted to stand out in our class, but there comes the time that taught me to be with others, not just to be among others. This is why despite of my position, I always try my best to be fit with others. I am actually lowering myself just to be with them. That is one of the lessons that my experience and my mother taught me indirectly. This is to blend with others and not just to be a killjoy to everyone.
